❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload notes?

1. Login to your account 2. Go to "Upload Notes" tab 3. Fill in title & category 4. Select file 5. Click Upload

What file types are allowed?

PDF, DOC, DOCX, and TXT files up to 20MB. PDF is recommended for best compatibility.

Can I edit notes after uploading?

Currently, you can view your uploads in your profile. To edit, please re-upload a corrected version.

What are categories for?

Categories help organize notes by subject. Choose the most relevant one so students can find your notes easily.

How do I download notes from others?

Click on any note in the "Browse" tab, read reviews, and click the download button.

Is there a limit on uploads?

No limit! Upload as many notes as you want. The more you share, the more the community grows!
